veAEV Governance & Locking Mechanism

Staking Mechanics​

Operational Staking​

Execution Nodes and Contributor Nodes must stake $AEV as a performance bond against Slashing Risks (e.g., malicious behavior or downtime).

This grants network access, but explicitly separates labor from capital control—staking alone does not confer governance rights or $AUV minting capabilities.

Governance Locking​

Governance power is derived exclusively from Governance Locking:

  1. Users time-lock $AEV to receive $veAEV
  2. Longer lock periods yield more veAEV
  3. veAEV decays over time as lock period expires

veAEV Benefits​

$veAEV holders can:

  • Govern: Vote on protocol parameters, treasury allocation, and content consensus
  • Mint: Produce $AUV based on veAEV holdings
  • Boost: Receive enhanced rewards through contribution multipliers

Anti-Plutocracy Design​

The protocol uses a logarithmic weighting formula to mitigate plutocracy:

  • Massive capital accumulation yields diminishing returns in voting power
  • Ensures smaller contributors maintain meaningful voice
  • Balances capital influence with time commitment
