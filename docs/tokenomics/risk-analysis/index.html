Parameter Sensitivity & System Risks

Basic Risk Management Principles​

1. Diversify Risk​

  • Don't put everything into one role/strategy (all-lock, all-stake, etc.)
  • Separate funds by purpose (trading, long-term, ops bond)

2. Informed Participation​

  • Understand Aevir's architecture, tokenomics, and governance before committing
  • Track proposals and community updates

3. Security Over Yield​

  • Operators and large participants should prioritize safety and stability
  • Don't chase short-term yield at the expense of long-term security

Parameter Change Impact​

DAO governance may change key parameters affecting:

  • Staking yields
  • ICN/content node payback periods
  • Sustainability of certain app models

Mitigation Strategies​

  • Stay informed about governance proposals
  • Participate in voting to influence parameter changes
  • Maintain flexibility in participation strategy
  • Keep reserves for unexpected changes
