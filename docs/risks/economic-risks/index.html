Economic Risks

This is not investment or legal advice; it's for disclosure and education only.

1. Price Volatility​

As a freely traded crypto asset, $AEV can:

  • Swing sharply in the short term
  • Move with market sentiment, macro conditions, and industry news

Participation modes (holding $AEV, staking for nodes, locking for $veAEV) all face P&L swings.

Practices:

  • Don't commit more than you can afford to lose
  • Understand how lockup/unlock cycles affect liquidity
  • Separate long-term participation from short-term price expectations

2. Liquidity Risk​

  • Early or stressed markets may have shallow depth for $AEV
  • Large trades may incur big slippage
  • Lockups and unbonding (e.g., 14 days) add liquidity discount

Practices:

  • Plan lockup size/term carefully
  • Operators: keep some "emergency liquidity"
  • Large participants: know main venues and depth

3. Tokenomics / Parameter Change Risk​

  • Real-world performance can differ from modeled assumptions
  • DAO governance may change key parameters (rewards, fees)

Practices:

  • Know current parameters and possible future changes
  • Follow proposals and discussions; vote when relevant
  • Don't assume any parameter is "forever"
